Chicken Tikka

Description

Chicken Tikka is a popular Indian dish made with marinated chicken pieces, grilled or roasted until tender and juicy. The marinade is made from yogurt, spices, and herbs, giving it a rich flavor. It's typically served with naan, rice, or as an appetizer with a side of chutney.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 3 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger garlic paste, lemon juice, and spices to make the marinade.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the marinade and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven or grill to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Skewer the marinated chicken pieces and cook for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Serve with naan, rice, or a side of chutney.
